Miles of Connection

Loving you from a distance has taught me something beautiful — that love isn’t measured in miles, touches, or how often we meet, but in how deeply we stay connected even when the world keeps us apart. Every sunrise without you makes me miss you a little more, but it also makes me appreciate us a little deeper. So until the day I can place a rose in your hands and see your smile right in front of me, I send you these small pieces of my heart instead — through words, through memories, through this little world I created just for you. No matter how far the distance stretches, my love will always find its way back to you.
